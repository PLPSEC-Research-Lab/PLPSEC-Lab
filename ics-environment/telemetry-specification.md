# Telemetry Specification
 
## Temperature
 
Description:
 
Water temperature measurement.
 
Normal Range:
 
15–30 °C
 
Example Values:
 
* 18.5 °C
* 22.0 °C
* 26.3 °C
 
---
 
## Pressure
 
Description:
 
Process pressure measurement.
 
Normal Range:
 
2–6 bar
 
Example Values:
 
* 2.5 bar
* 3.8 bar
* 5.2 bar
 
---
 
## Chlorine
 
Description:
 
Chlorine concentration monitoring.
 
Normal Range:
 
0.2–1.5 mg/L
 
Example Values:
 
* 0.4 mg/L
* 0.8 mg/L
* 1.2 mg/L
 
---
 
## Pump Status
 
Possible States:
 
* ON
* OFF
 
Purpose:
 
Indicates operational state of the process pump.
